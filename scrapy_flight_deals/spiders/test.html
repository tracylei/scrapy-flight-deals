Toronto to Turks and Caicos - $263 CAD roundtrip including taxes


Air Canada Rouge has dropped the price of their February and March flights from Toronto to the beautiful Caribbean island of Turks & Caicos.

Here's how to find this deal...

1. Go to the Air Canada Vacations website (you won't see them on AirCanada.com because they are Air Canada charter flights)

2. Click on the 'Flights' tab under Vacation Search
^^ very important, a lot of people miss this, and think they can't find the deal.

3. Search for a flight from Toronto (YYZ) to Providenciales (PLS) , Turks & Caicos

Where to stay in Turks & Caicos?

The following AirBNB links will lead you to some great deals on places to stay in Turks & Caicos for under $149 CAD/night for 2 people.

AirBNB is showing around 21 listings for under $149 CAD/night for 2 people.

AirBNB in Turks & Caicos - 21 listings for under $149 CAD/night

You could also try VRBO, which is claiming there are 12 properties for under $125 USD/night. Just run a search for Turks & Caicos when you get there. After you hit Search you can apply the price filter.
